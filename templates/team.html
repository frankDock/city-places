Marten Lunge

Founder & CEO

Originally from Tartu, Estonia - he grew accustomed to different cultures at a young age, having moved to Luxembourg to finish his last four years of secondary school there. He moved on to England to enrol at the Warwick Business School and met with even more great people from all over the world. After finishing his studies he returned to Estonia and soon joined a professional SaaS start-up Weekdone that helps team managers around the globe to manage their teams better. He was part of helping Weekdone reach profitability ahead of schedule, despite being the only person at the business development team for a year. With Weekdone and a previous project, he has over six years of experience with start-ups. His true passion has always been travelling and Elleday enables to improve travelling even more and create a great product for travellers out there!

Aare Puussaar

Chief Technology Officer

Aare has over eight years of experience as a developer and his current passion lies in data science - in understanding the relationship between data, information and knowledge. He was motivated to pursue his doctorate at the Newcastle University, to explore how we can configure technology to serve the needs of the citizens and how to provide people the tools to reclaim the ownership of their data. Before starting his doctorate, he was the development lead with Positium - a company focusing on location-based insights through mobile data. He used the power of Hadoop to build a framework for extracting meaningful insights from passive mobile positioning data.
