Dare to Moderate

Shine a light and karma your way to the moderator status!

Help with your reviews and ratings and in return earn karma points. To show our gratitude in thanks to helping to create a better city guide for everyone - we reward our moderators by sharing more than a quarter of our earnings from their respective cities!

The game itself is easy. There are five levels, that show different user status. You get to the next level by collecting a required amount of karma points. At the ultimate level you will become a moderator and will qualify for the revenue share with all the other moderators in the city, where you earned enough karma. We share 25 per cent of our city revenue with all the moderators who qualified.

There’s also a special bonus for the Top 3 users! The person who earned the most karma in a given month will be rewarded to keep 5% of the earnings from their respective city, with the second place earning an extra 3% and the third will earn 2%.

We make the revenue we earned in the last 3 months in a city public. We’re big believers in transparency and have no problem with opening our books. When we’re starting out in a city and you notice that we’re not making a lot - well, you can laugh at us, tell your friends and laugh some more, even go online, post about it, maybe create a Reddit circlejerk and really go at it. Or, you can contact our team at feedback@elleday.com and share your thoughts on how, we can increase our earnings - and help to create a larger income pool that we can share among the moderators. You can even get in touch at team@elleday.com and become our city representative, to help us reach more businesses in your city!

So how do we make money? And what’s included in the city revenue? We charge businesses listed at the featured spots on the front page and for the premium features they can purchase. While we will potentially add a few more revenue sources, currently these make-up the city revenue. This means that, when more businesses buy featured spots and premium features, the higher their respective city earnings will be.

What type of feedback is really helpful? You can leave suggestions for how to reach more businesses in your city or give suggestions for new paid features for businesses.
